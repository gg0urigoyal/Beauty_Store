✦ Product Details

Natural
Purification Ritual

Pure Green Clay Mask harnesses the power of natural French green clay to draw out impurities and excess oil. Enriched with soothing botanicals, it leaves skin feeling clean, balanced, and refreshed without stripping moisture.

French Green Clay (Illite), Aqua (Water), Glycerin, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, and natural preservatives.

Apply a thin layer to clean, dry skin avoiding the eye area. Leave on for 10-15 minutes until dry, then rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. Use 1-2 times per week.

Users appreciate the gentle yet effective cleansing action. The mask is praised for reducing breakouts and leaving skin feeling soft and balanced.

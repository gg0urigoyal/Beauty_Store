✦ Product Details

The Midnight
Bloom Experience

Midnight Bloom Eau de Parfum unfolds like a secret garden at dusk. Top notes of night-blooming jasmine and moonflower give way to a heart of deep rose and velvet musk, finishing with warm amber and sandalwood.

Alcohol Denat, Parfum (Fragrance), Aqua (Water), Limonene, Linalool, Benzyl Benzoate, Citral, Geraniol, Eugenol, and other aromatic compounds.

Spray on pulse points: wrists, neck, and behind ears. For evening wear, apply to inner elbows and knees for a subtle trail. Reapply as desired throughout the evening.

Customers describe it as sophisticated and alluring. The scent evolves beautifully throughout the night, making it perfect for special occasions and romantic evenings.

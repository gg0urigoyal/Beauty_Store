✦ Product Details

Formula Notes &
Application Ritual

Radiance Renewal Serum is designed to target dullness, uneven tone, and dehydration in one step. The texture absorbs quickly, layers beautifully under moisturizer, and leaves skin fresh, luminous, and comfortable throughout the day.

Vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, niacinamide, aloe vera extract, licorice root extract, green tea extract, and hydration-supporting humectants blended for brightening and moisture retention.

Apply 2 to 3 drops to clean skin after cleansing and toning. Massage over the face and neck, then follow with moisturizer. Use sunscreen during the day for best results.

Customers consistently mention the lightweight feel, hydrated finish, and visible brightening over time. It is especially loved as a daily glow-boosting serum under makeup.
